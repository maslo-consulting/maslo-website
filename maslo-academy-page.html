Frequently asked questions
What colleges and students ask us
Common questions from placement officers, HODs, and students considering Maslo Academy.

Can Maslo Academy programmes be embedded into our college curriculum?

Yes — this is our preferred model for institutional partnerships. Maslo Academy programmes are designed to be modular and flexible, so colleges can embed individual tracks (coding, AI, communication, placement prep) as electives, integrate them into final year programmes, or offer the full campus readiness curriculum as a mandatory pre-placement programme. We work with your academic calendar and delivery infrastructure — on-campus sessions, online access, or a blended model.

What is Campus Career and how does it benefit our students?

Campus Career is Maslo's AI-powered campus recruitment marketplace. Students who complete Maslo Academy programmes are profiled on Campus Career with verified assessment scores — aptitude, technical, soft skills, communication, and personality. Recruiters from companies across India browse these profiles, shortlist based on objective scores, and connect directly with students — skipping the traditional placement office process. For students, this means recruiter visibility beyond their own college's placement drive. For colleges, it means additional placement opportunities through Maslo's recruiter network.

Do students need prior coding experience for the coding bootcamp?

No. The coding bootcamp is designed for students with little to no prior coding experience. It starts from absolute fundamentals — what programming is, how to think computationally — before progressing through Python or Java syntax, data structures, and problem-solving. Students who already have some exposure to coding can join at an intermediate level. The bootcamp is structured so students at different starting points can participate effectively.

What is Languapro and how is it different from a regular English class?

Languapro is Maslo's proprietary AI-powered communication assessment and learning platform — not a classroom English course. The key difference is that Languapro starts with an objective AI assessment of each student's current LSRW (Listening, Speaking, Reading, Writing) proficiency, identifies specific gaps, and then delivers personalised learning content to address those gaps. Progress is measured at multiple points using AI, not subjective trainer evaluation. At completion, students receive a communication proficiency certificate with specific skill scores — meaningful to employers who want to know exactly how well a candidate communicates.

How is the AI & ML programme relevant for non-computer science students?

The AI & ML fundamentals programme is specifically designed to be accessible to students from any engineering or science discipline — not just computer science. AI is increasingly applied in every domain — manufacturing, healthcare, finance, logistics, agriculture — and employers across all sectors are looking for graduates who understand AI concepts and can work alongside AI systems. The programme requires no prior ML knowledge and uses Python as the programming language, which most engineering students can pick up quickly. The certificate demonstrates genuine AI literacy that is valued by employers across all domains.
